Why Order Samples First?

Smart Planning: Colors look different in sun vs. shade, and on different base surfaces. Ordering samples first lets you plan with confidence before committing to a bulk material order.

Important: Final results depend on base condition, weather, mixing, and installation quality. For larger patios, pool decks, driveways, or commercial projects, consult a qualified installer.
