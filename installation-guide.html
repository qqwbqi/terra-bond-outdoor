Installation Guide

Important preparation, application, and safety information for bonded stone outdoor surfaces.

Disclaimer: Final results depend on base condition, weather, mixing, and installation quality. For larger patios, pool decks, driveways, or commercial projects, consult a qualified installer. Samples are for color and texture review only.

Step-by-Step Installation

Surface Preparation

Base must be clean, dry, and structurally sound. Remove all loose material, oil, grease, and debris. Repair cracks and level uneven areas. The surface should be completely dry before application.

Weather Check

Apply only in dry conditions with temperatures above 50°F (10°C) and no rain forecast for 24 hours. Avoid direct hot sun if possible — early morning is ideal.

Mixing

Mix resin and hardener exactly per the provided ratio. Add decorative stone and mix thoroughly until all stone is evenly coated. Do not add extra resin or hardener.

Application

Pour mixture onto the prepared surface. Use a trowel to spread evenly to the desired thickness. Work quickly — pot life is limited. Ensure edges are clean and smooth.

Curing

Allow 24 hours before light foot traffic. Full cure may take 48–72 hours depending on temperature and humidity. Keep the area dry during curing.

DIY vs. Professional Installation

Small DIY Projects

Areas under 50 sq ft with good base conditions may be suitable for confident DIYers. Order a DIY Test Kit first to practice technique.

Professional Recommended

For patios over 100 sq ft, pool decks, driveways, or commercial projects, we strongly recommend a qualified installer. Request a project estimate for installer referrals.
