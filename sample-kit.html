FAQ

What can I do with the samples? +
Samples are for color and texture review only. They help you compare colors in your actual outdoor light and against your existing surfaces before planning a bulk material order.
How long does shipping take? +
Sample kits ship to the U.S. in 7–12 days. You will receive a tracking number once your order is dispatched.
Can I order bulk materials after receiving samples? +
Yes! After reviewing your samples, submit a Project Estimate with your photos, area, and timeline. Bulk material orders are quoted separately based on your project requirements.
Are the samples refundable? +
Samples are for review purposes. If your kit arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours with photos for a replacement.
