Shipping & Returns

Clear policies for sample kits, DIY test kits, and bulk material orders.

Shipping

Sample Kits

Ships within 1–2 business days. Estimated delivery to the U.S.: 7–12 days. Free shipping on orders over $50.

DIY Test Kits

Ships within 1–2 business days. Estimated delivery to the U.S.: 7–12 days. Free shipping on orders over $50.

Bulk Materials

Shipping is calculated separately as part of your custom quote. Lead times depend on project location and material volume. We work with freight partners to optimize delivery.

Damage Policy: If your kit arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours with photos. We will arrange a replacement at no charge.

Returns

Sample Kits

Unused kits can be returned within 14 days of delivery. Original packaging must be intact. Customer is responsible for return shipping unless the item arrived damaged.

DIY Test Kits

Unused kits can be returned within 14 days of delivery. Once opened or used, test kits are not eligible for return.

Bulk Materials

Bulk material orders are custom quoted and prepared for your specific project. Once materials are prepared or shipped, they are not eligible for standard returns. Please order samples and confirm your project details before committing to a bulk order.
