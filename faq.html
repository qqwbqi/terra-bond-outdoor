Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about samples, shipping, DIY, bulk orders, and installation.

What is included in the Sample Kit? +
The 20-Color Sample Kit includes real cured bonded stone samples in 20 curated colors, organized in a labeled box with color names and recommended usage scenes. Samples are for color and texture review only.
How long does shipping take? +
Sample kits ship to the U.S. in 7–12 business days. DIY Test Kits follow the same timeline. You will receive a tracking number once your order is dispatched.
Can I install this myself? +
Small areas under 50 sq ft with good base conditions may be suitable for confident DIYers. We recommend ordering a DIY Test Kit first to practice. For larger patios, pool decks, driveways, or commercial projects, consult a qualified installer.
How do I order bulk materials? +
Bulk material orders are quoted separately. Submit a Project Estimate with your photos, area, and timeline. We'll review and respond within 24–48 hours with a custom quote.
What is your return policy? +
Sample kits and DIY test kits can be returned unused within 14 days. If your kit arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours with photos for a replacement. Bulk material orders are custom quoted and not eligible for standard returns once materials are prepared for shipment.
Do the colors look the same in person? +
Colors may vary depending on lighting conditions, screen settings, and base surface. This is exactly why we recommend ordering samples first — compare them in your actual outdoor light before planning a bulk order.
What surfaces can this be applied to? +
Bonded stone surfaces may be suitable for concrete, asphalt, and stable paver bases depending on project conditions. Base must be clean, dry, and structurally sound. Final suitability depends on the specific condition of your existing surface.
Do you offer contractor referrals? +
Yes, we can connect qualified installers in your area. Include this request in your Project Estimate or contact us directly.
